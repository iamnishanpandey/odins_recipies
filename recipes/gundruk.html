Gundruk (Fermented Leafy Greens)

Description

Gundruk is a traditional Nepali side dish made by fermenting leafy greens, usually mustard greens or spinach. The fermentation process gives it a distinctive sour flavor. Gundruk is commonly used in soups, stews, or as a side dish, adding a unique taste to Nepali cuisine.

ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped mustard greens or spinach
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Instructions

  1. Mix chopped greens with turmeric and salt.
  2. Pack the mixture tightly in a jar or container.
  3. Leave it to ferment in the sun for 3-4 days.
  4. Gundruk is ready when it has a slightly sour taste.

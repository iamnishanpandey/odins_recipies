Dal Bhat (Lentil Soup with Rice)

Description

Dal Bhat is the staple food of Nepal, featuring lentil soup (dal) served with rice (bhat). The lentils are seasoned with turmeric, cumin seeds, and other spices, providing a nutritious and satisfying meal. It's often accompanied by vegetable curries, pickles, and sometimes meat dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils (red or yellow per person)
  • 1 cup rice(per person)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils and cook with turmeric and salt until soft.
  2. In a separate pan, heat mustard oil, add cumin seeds, onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Mix cooked lentils with the sautéed mixture.
  5. Serve hot with rice.

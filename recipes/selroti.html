Sel Roti (Traditional Rice Doughnut)

Description

Sel Roti is a traditional rice-based doughnut that is popular during festivals and special occasions. The batter is made with rice flour and is deep-fried to achieve a crispy exterior and a soft, chewy interior. It is often enjoyed with yogurt or traditional Nepali pickles.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix rice flour, milk, water, and baking soda to make a smooth batter.
  2. Let the batter rest for 4-5 hours.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, pour the batter in a circular motion to form a doughnut shape.
  4. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
